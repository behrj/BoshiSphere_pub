# Privacy Policy for BoshiSphere

## General Information
BoshiSphere is a fully offline app designed to calculate crochet patterns for round projects. Protecting your data is important to us. For this reason, the app **does not collect, store, or process any personal data**.

---

## Data Collected
BoshiSphere:

- **does not collect any personal data**,  
- **does not store any data**,  
- **does not transmit any data to third parties**,  
- **does not use analytics or tracking services**.

All inputs (e.g., stitch width, stitch height, circumference) are processed **locally on your device** and are never recorded or shared.

---

## Advertising
BoshiSphere displays **no advertisements** and does not use any advertising networks.

---

## Permissions
The app requires **no special permissions** and does not access any device data or system features.
